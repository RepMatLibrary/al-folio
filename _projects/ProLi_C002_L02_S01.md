<h4><b>Applications</b></h4>
<h5>Sports - Protective elements</h5>

<br>Customizable protective equipment (shin guards) for sports and recreational activities.
<br>This 3D model is a part (shield detail) of a customizable <b>shin guard</b> for sports and recreational activities. The original concept has been developed during the Ph.D. research period abroad of Alessia Romani at FAST Lab (Western University).
