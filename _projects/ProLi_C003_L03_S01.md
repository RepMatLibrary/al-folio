<h4><b>Applications</b></h4>
<h5>Music - Customized technical components</h5>

<br>Customizable and reparable technical components of a guitar neck for 3D printing.
<br>This 3D model is a cut-off (headstock detail) of Ecdisi, a modular guitar neck to be produced with additive manufacturing technologies, following the principles of circular design (reuse, repair, refurbish). The original concept has been developed during the Master Thesis in Design & Engineering of Davide Teruzzi (Politecnico di Milano).
